Our Management Team

At Khaana, leadership is about vision, responsibility, and passion. Our team combines decades of experience in foodservice, technology, and logistics to deliver healthy meals to your doorstep.

Jane Smith – Chief Executive Officer

Jane founded Khaana in 2020 with a mission to make ready-to-eat meal delivery convenient, affordable, and sustainable for every customer. With over 20 years of experience in hospitality management and entrepreneurship, she ensures that Khaana maintains its commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and community partnerships. Jane oversees company strategy, marketing initiatives, financial planning, and long-term growth, while mentoring the leadership team to achieve operational excellence across every department.

  • Over 20 years in hospitality and business management
  • Leads partnerships with local suppliers
  • Guides company vision and leadership

Brian Jones – Chief Technology Officer

Brian spearheads Khaana’s technological vision, designing innovative solutions to enhance the meal ordering experience and optimize delivery logistics. He leads software development, IT infrastructure, and system security initiatives, ensuring that technology empowers both employees and customers. Brian consistently evaluates emerging technologies, integrating AI and data analytics to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and maintain a competitive edge in the food delivery industry.

Our Management Team

At Khaana, leadership is about vision, responsibility, and passion. Our team combines decades of experience in foodservice, technology, and logistics to deliver healthy meals to your doorstep.

1. Jane Smith – Chief Executive Officer

Jane founded Khaana in 2020 with the goal of revolutionizing meal delivery services by combining convenience, health, and sustainability. She oversees the overall strategic direction of the company, ensuring all departments work cohesively to deliver high-quality meals while maintaining strong partnerships with suppliers and stakeholders.

  • Over 20 years in hospitality and business management
  • Leads partnerships with local suppliers
  • Guides company vision and leadership

2. Brian Jones – Chief Technology Officer

Brian drives innovation at Khaana, overseeing the design and implementation of digital platforms that allow customers to easily order meals and track deliveries. He works to integrate cutting-edge technology with logistics to optimize delivery times and customer satisfaction, while ensuring data security across the platform.

  1. Callie Hekter – IT Security Manager

    Callie manages the company's cybersecurity strategy, protecting sensitive customer and company data from threats. She develops protocols, conducts audits, and trains staff to respond effectively to security challenges, ensuring Khaana's digital operations remain safe and reliable.

    Cody Weinman - Network Security Specialist. Cody maintains the company’s network infrastructure, identifying vulnerabilities and implementing solutions to prevent potential breaches, while ensuring smooth operation of all IT systems.

    Lauren Daniels - Compliance & Risk Analyst. Lauren monitors regulatory compliance and manages risk assessments, helping the team stay aligned with legal and industry standards while mitigating operational risks.

  2. Sydney Arias – Software Development Manager

    Sydney leads all software development projects, ensuring the company’s app and website are intuitive, fast, and reliable. She coordinates development teams, implements new features, and ensures that all software improvements enhance the overall customer experience.

    Emily Miller - Front-End Developer. Emily designs and maintains the user interface of the Khaana platform, ensuring a seamless and visually appealing experience for all users.

    Ja'Kaila Clay - Back-End Developer. Ja'Kaila manages server-side logic, databases, and integrations, ensuring reliable and secure data flow across the system.

3. Jennifer Brown – Head of Operations

Jennifer manages the day-to-day operations of Khaana, from kitchen management to delivery logistics. She ensures every meal meets the highest quality standards, oversees staffing and scheduling, and continuously improves operational efficiency across the company.

  • Manages logistics, kitchen staff, and quality assurance
  • Oversees scheduling, food preparation, and packaging

Brooke Young – Logistics Manager

Brooke coordinates delivery routes, manages drivers, and ensures timely distribution of meals. She monitors fleet maintenance and continuously optimizes logistics operations to meet customer expectations.

Kristin Bahr - Delivery Supervisor. Kristin supervises all delivery personnel, monitors route efficiency, and ensures meals are delivered promptly and safely.

Ryan James - Route Planner. Ryan analyzes and plans efficient delivery routes, reducing travel time and enhancing overall logistics efficiency.

Lance Alston – Kitchen Manager

Lance supervises the kitchen staff, manages meal preparation processes, and ensures all food safety and quality standards are met. He constantly reviews workflows for efficiency and maintains high-quality culinary standards.

Francesca Demorizi - Head Chef. Francesca leads the culinary team, develops recipes, and ensures consistency in taste and presentation of all meals.

Alexander Bull - Prep Supervisor. Alexander organizes ingredient preparation, manages prep staff, and ensures kitchen readiness for daily meal production.

Colin Freeman – Quality Assurance Manager

Colin oversees product quality, implements compliance checks, and develops process improvements to maintain high standards throughout Khaana's operations. His team ensures customer satisfaction through consistent quality monitoring.

Mollie Parker - QA Specialist. Mollie performs routine quality inspections, identifies areas for improvement, and reports findings to management.

Charlotte Mansur - Compliance Officer. Charlotte ensures all company operations adhere to regulations and internal policies, mitigating risks and maintaining compliance standards.

Our Management Team

At Khaana, leadership is about vision, responsibility, and passion. Our team combines decades of experience in foodservice, technology, and logistics to deliver healthy meals to your doorstep.

Jane Smith – Chief Executive Officer

Jane founded Khaana in 2020 to make ready-to-eat meal delivery accessible and affordable. She oversees company strategy, marketing, and finance, ensuring sustainable growth and fostering a culture of innovation, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Jane works closely with all department heads to mentor emerging leaders and maintain strategic partnerships with key suppliers.

  • Over 20 years in hospitality and business management
  • Leads partnerships with local suppliers
  • Guides company vision and leadership

Brian Jones – Chief Technology Officer

Brian drives Khaana’s technology innovations, overseeing our ordering platform, delivery optimization, and digital strategy. He leads software development, cybersecurity, and IT infrastructure teams, integrating advanced technologies to ensure operational efficiency and secure customer experiences. Brian also mentors his team and evaluates new tools to maintain high standards and innovation across all digital initiatives.

Jennifer Brown – Head of Operations

Jennifer manages Khaana’s daily operations, focusing on logistics, kitchen efficiency, and delivery accuracy. She implements operational strategies, optimizes workflows, maintains compliance with food safety standards, and mentors staff to foster accountability and excellence.

  • Oversees scheduling, food preparation, and packaging

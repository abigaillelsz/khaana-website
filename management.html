Our Management Team

At Khaana, leadership is about vision, responsibility, and passion. Our team combines decades of experience in foodservice, technology, and logistics to deliver healthy meals to your doorstep.

Jane Smith – Chief Executive Officer

Jane founded Khaana in 2020 to make ready-to-eat meal delivery accessible and affordable. She oversees company strategy, marketing, and finance, ensuring that the organization grows sustainably while staying true to its mission. Jane collaborates closely with all department heads, mentoring emerging leaders and fostering a company culture that values innovation, integrity, and customer satisfaction. She maintains strategic relationships with key partners and suppliers to optimize operations and ensure the highest standards of quality and service.

  • Over 20 years in hospitality and business management
  • Leads partnerships with local suppliers
  • Guides company vision and leadership

Brian Jones – Chief Technology Officer

Brian drives Khaana’s technology innovations, overseeing our ordering platform and delivery optimization. He is responsible for implementing advanced IT systems that ensure seamless digital experiences for both customers and staff. Brian leads software development, cybersecurity, and infrastructure teams, constantly seeking ways to improve efficiency and scalability. His expertise in emerging technologies allows Khaana to stay ahead of competitors and adopt solutions that enhance operational effectiveness, user experience, and data-driven decision-making.

Jennifer focuses on continuous process improvement, quality control, and compliance with food safety standards.
